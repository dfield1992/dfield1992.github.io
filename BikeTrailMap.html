The Oregon Bike Trail

The Oregon Bike Trail Map was created in the fall of 2018 during one of my Master's class known as Graphic Design in Cartography. The purpose of the lab assignment was to enhance terrain representation by editing your map products with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. As you can see in the map the hillshade is poping out of the screen giving it that curvy hillshade look.

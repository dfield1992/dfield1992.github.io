Wisconsin Tribal Lands Map

This is a map I created showing the 11 tribal nations in the State of Wisconsin. When this project was first assigned to me I thought it would be a very simple to do map product. In reality this map turned into one of the more complex maps I've created. As you can see the tribal lands are sparse meaning they are dispersered throughout the state making it difficult to map. With the mission to produce a statewide tribal lands map I had to think outside of the box by editing the legend symbology and creating an inset map. This map is now widely used throughout our organization to better ensure we include every tribinal nation in emergency preparedness and respone.
